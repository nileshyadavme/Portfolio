# Hello World

Welcome to my new portfolio! This project has been a labor of love, capable of showcasing not just my work, but also my personality and passion for software engineering.

## The Tech Stack

I chose **Next.js** for its performance and developer experience. The App Router has been a game-changer for organizing my code.

// Example of a simple component

## Design Philosophy

I wanted a design that felt "mathematical" and "space-themed". The dark mode, the shooting stars, and the precise typography all contribute to this aesthetic.

## Challenges

One of the biggest challenges was getting the animations to feel "organic" without killing performance. I used **Framer Motion** for the UI elements and **HTML5 Canvas** for the background stars to ensure a smooth 60fps experience.
